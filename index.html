Before you explore the wonders of this game there is a little secret I gotta tell you. The main objective of the game is to get a high rating by the time the day ends which is 5pm (18 minutes in realtime). There are a few tips that I think you should follow: try to serve the customers as fast as you can while also getting their order right! The less time they wait, the better your rating. Try to utilize the plates on the counter to store unwanted items instead of throwing them out immediently. If your trashcan gets full then you'll have to empty it mid-shift! Emptying a trashcan midshift can lead to a reduced rating or a slower production time! Well, that's all I've got. Good luck cooking!
